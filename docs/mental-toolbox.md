## Git & GitHub

- **GitHub** = My office lobby, where I professionally present my wokr and where people discover DC Decision Science
- **Repository** = My Client's file, everything needed to analyze and solve their problems lives here.
- **Workspace** = my office desk, I could have multiple client binders open here while I am working. It is my job to ensure I am in the corrent one
- **Clone** = checking out a copy of a library book so I can have it anywhere.
- **README** = note to future self(The reception desk). It tells visitors what they're looking at and why it matters.
- **Git** = My time machine. Ever commit creates a save point that can be returned to later if needed!
