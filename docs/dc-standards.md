# The Operating Standards of DC Decision Science


## Consulting Principle
 - Seek to understand the operational context before analyzing the data.

---
## The DCDS Investigation Framework
When performance changes...

Ask these questions **in order**.

1. What changed?
- Business Context

2. How did the supporting systems change?
- Policies
- Training
- Technology
- Staffing
- Leadership
- Communication
- Safety

---
## DC Standard 001

- Ever case study must produce actionable recommendations that improve operational performance while maintaining or improving safety.
